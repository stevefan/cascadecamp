Cascadia · forming now

A small camp for belonging and collaboration.

Cascade Camp is a participant-produced gathering for people from an online network to meet slowly, make things, host what they care about, and practice being a village for a weekend.

Get updates hello@cascade.camp

What this is

A laboratory for belonging and collaboration. Not a polished conference. Not a passive retreat. A light container where attendees bring the sessions, projects, meals, walks, questions, rituals, and experiments that make the weekend real.

The starting point is simple: online relationships need places to become embodied. Camp gives those relationships time, weather, dishes, shared attention, and enough structure to become more real.

At camp

  • Recharge offline.
  • Meet mutuals in a slow, cozy setting.
  • Share something you love or try something new.
  • Help with the weekend: kitchen, welcome, cleanup, recording, logistics.
  • Leave with stronger relationships and clearer next invitations.
